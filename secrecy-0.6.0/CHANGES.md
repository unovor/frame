## [0.6.0] (2019-12-12)

- Impl `CloneableSecret` for `Secret<[T; N]>` where `T: Clone` ([#311])
- Impl `DebugSecret` for `[T; N]` where `N` <= 64 ([#310])
- Impl `FromStr` for `SecretString` ([#309])
- Upgrade to `bytes` v0.5 ([#301], [#308], [#312])
